Radiant Promises

Radiant Promises is a non-profit organization designed to circumvent homelessness and hopelessness in at-risk youth. The program provides housing and empowering education for young adults ages 18-24 who have aged out of the foster care system and youth who have been rescued from sex-trafficking in need of both a home, healing and a fresh new start in life.
